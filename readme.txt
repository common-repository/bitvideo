== Description == 

The BitVideo is the best way to increase how often your readers share your content. It creates higher user engagement for viewers to your WordPress site. While watching the video, viewers will see six different share buttons on the screen, encouraging engagement and driving more viewers to your site.

The BitWall video share plugin creates higher user engagement for viewers to your WordPress site. While watching the video, viewers will see six different share buttons on the screen, encouraging engagement and driving more viewers to your site.

The plugin is easy to install, and requires no setting adjustments to adapt to your blog.

Once installed the buttons form fit around your YouTube video and provide viewers with instant access to engage. 

For more on BitWall, visit [bitwall.io](http://www.bitwall.io "BitWall's Homepage")

== Installation == 

1.  In your WordPress dashboard, go to Plugins-> Add New -> install "BitVideo".
2.  Activate BitWall through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3.  While creating a new post check to enable a widget in the BitWall metabox.
4.  You will need to activate your Twitter account here: https://dev.twitter.com/docs/cards/validation/validator
5.  After you are signed in with Twitter above, select "card validated for twitter summary_large_image"
